Digital Classrooms — Learning Without Borders

Offline-capable lessons, local-language content, and a simple teacher dashboard — designed for low-end devices and poor connectivity.

Why this platform?

Many government schools in Nabha and nearby rural areas lack updated computers, stable internet, and quality digital resources. This platform provides offline lessons, simple interactive quizzes, local-language content, and a teacher dashboard to track student progress.

Impact

  • Bridges the rural–urban digital divide.
  • Builds foundational digital literacy.
  • Equips students for future learning and employability.
